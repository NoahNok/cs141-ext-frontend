LAC Solver

© Noah Hollowell 2021

This interface allows you to construct a grid from 1x1 up to 10x10 (currently limited to 5x5) that the Large Arithmetic Collider will solve for you and show you how.
Note that some may have thousands of possible ways to solve, so they may take along time to compute and return!
